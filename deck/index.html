The Cultural Shift

OONTZ HAS ALWAYS HAD ENERGY.
SANDILE IS MAKING IT SEXY.

Across Nairobi, Johannesburg, Lagos and Cape Town, a new generation of nightlife audiences is emerging — culturally aware, digitally expressive, fashion-conscious and experience-driven. House music — what Nairobi's community knows as oontz — has always carried raw, communal energy. A dance floor full of people losing themselves in the groove. Sandile is not here to remove that energy; Sandile is here to dress it. Afrohouse and Amapiano have become social languages. The dance floor has become a cultural meeting point.

The Alignment

BOTH BUILT ON
THE SAME
BELIEF.

Martell's positioning naturally aligns with the Sandile experience. Both represent sophistication, craftsmanship, elevated taste, and intentional experience design.

Martell has spent over 300 years building something rare — a legacy that feels both timeless and alive. Sandile is building something in real time: a cultural platform that feels both rooted and forward-facing.

After Hours creates a space where Martell can exist not as an advertisement, but as part of the emotional architecture of the night itself.
